About Acceler8

The advisor who has sat on both sides of the table

Acceler8 Commercial was built on a simple observation: most commercial consultants come from one background — sales or procurement. Rarely both.

Founder Adam Solley has spent twenty years at the most senior levels of each. He has built and led Procurement and Commercial functions from the ground up, and personally negotiated and closed over 250 commercial sales deals worth more than £240m TCV — across enterprise software, professional services and partnership agreements.

That dual perspective shapes everything Acceler8 does. Because understanding how buyers think, what they scrutinise, and where commercial friction originates isn't theory — it comes from having sat in both chairs.

Calm under pressure

When negotiations stall and stakes are high, Adam brings a steady, solutions-first approach that unlocks deals others walk away from.

Procurement & sales — both sides

A split career spanning procurement strategy, bid leadership and commercial director roles gives Acceler8 a perspective no pure-play sales consultant can match.

Embedded, not advisory

We work as an extension of your team — building processes, hiring into roles, drafting contracts, and leading negotiations alongside you.

Fintech & B2B SaaS specialism

Most businesses put lawyers in the room to negotiate contracts. Lawyers are trained to win every point — which slows deals, poisons relationships and kills commercial momentum. Acceler8 takes a different approach.

We draft, review and negotiate complex commercial contracts — MSAs, SaaS agreements, partnership and supplier terms — end-to-end, without legal counsel in the room.
