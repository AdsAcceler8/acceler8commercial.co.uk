Adam Solley

About Adam

A career built at the
commercial intersection

Adam began his career in management consulting, where he learned to diagnose complex business problems and design solutions across large organisations. But it was moving into procurement — at Brit Insurance, Equifax and then GSK — that gave him his deepest commercial education.

Procurement taught Adam how buyers really think. He holds a deep understanding of how they actually assess risk, challenge pricing, dissect contracts and decide what truly matters versus what they will concede. Most people who cross from procurement to sales lose that perspective quickly. Adam never did.

"The best commercial operators are not the ones who push hardest from one side of the table — they are the ones who understand both sides well enough to find a faster, more durable path to agreement."

When Adam joined Aptitude Software as Senior Commercial Director, he built their global commercial function from scratch — applying everything he had learned about how buyers behave to make it dramatically more effective. Ninety net-new deals. £120m in software contracts personally negotiated. Time to signature cut by 60%. Not by pushing harder, but by designing smarter processes, cleaner contracts and a commercial playbook that removed friction from every deal.

That experience is the foundation of Acceler8 Commercial. Every engagement is hands-on — Adam embeds into your team, works alongside your people, and leaves behind processes, playbooks and commercial capability that outlasts the engagement.

How Adam works

The approach behind the outcomes

01

Composed & decisive

Twenty years of high-stakes commercial negotiations has taught Adam that composure is a strategic asset. When deals become complex and pressure mounts, his instinct is to stay measured — reading the situation, identifying the real sticking points and cutting through the noise to accelerate towards signature. Steadiness at the table is not hesitancy; it is what gets deals closed when others lose their nerve.

02

Collaborative by instinct

Adam understands the power of the collective. Whether leading a team or contributing as a member, he actively seeks out input, challenge and creative thinking from those around him. The best commercial solutions are rarely built in isolation — and the best advice often comes from the least expected places.

03

A genuine role model

Adam takes pride in his work and believes you are only as good as your last engagement. That standard extends to the people he works with — sharing knowledge, passing on skills and helping clients and colleagues build lasting commercial capability alongside the immediate deliverable.
